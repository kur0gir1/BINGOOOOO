B I N G O

Rules:

How to play:

  • 1. B contains numbers 1 - 15
  • 2. I contains numbers 16 - 30
  • 3. N contains numbers 31 - 45
  • 4. G contains numbers 46 - 60
  • 5. O contains numbers 61 - 75
  • 6. Mark a number when it shows up on the number generator.
  • 7. NO CHEATING! Cheating is a sin.

How to win:

  • 1. The winner is the first person to cover any five spaces in a row; vertical, horizontal or diagonal in Bingo.
  • 2. When covering a space, whether vertically, horizontally, or diagonally, a letter shows up on the bottom. In order to win, 5 of the letters of B I N G O must appear.
  • 3. When the number is generated in the number generator, mark the number by clicking on it.
  • 4. To undo a mistake by accidentally clicking on the wrong number, simply click it again to invalidate the mistake.
  • 5. Once a space has been declared as a win and a letter shows up, it cannot be cancelled by clicking on the numbers again so be very careful.
  • 6. Enjoy the game and have fun! Kung mag daog ka may 10 pesos GCash ka kay sir Boky.
